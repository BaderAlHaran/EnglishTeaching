About English Essay Writing

A free essay checker and writing feedback site, built on a simple belief: every student deserves honest feedback on their writing, without paying for it.

Our Mission

English Essay Writing started with a simple idea: good feedback shouldn't cost money. Most grammar tools hide their best features behind subscriptions, and professional editing is priced out of reach for most students. This site offers both for free — an instant essay checker, and personal review by a real reader.

Every human-review submission is read personally — not by a bot or a template. The goal isn't to write essays for you — it's to show you what to fix and why, so your own writing gets stronger with every draft.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Honest Feedback

We point out what's actually wrong and how to fix it — no sugarcoating, no upselling. The goal is that you learn something from every check.

A Real Reader

Human review means exactly that: a real person reads your essay and responds personally — not a template, not an automated reply.

Privacy

Your essay stays yours. We never publish, share, or reuse your text — it's processed only to give you feedback.

Your Work, Improved

We don't write essays for you. We help you improve what you wrote — which is better for your grades and your integrity.
