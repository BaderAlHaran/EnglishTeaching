FAQ

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our free writing support service.

Is this really free?

Yes. EnglishEssayWriting.net is a free support service created to help people improve their writing. We do not charge fees, require subscriptions, or ask for payment details.

Who can use this service?

Anyone. This service is open to all levels, including high school, undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional writers. As long as the request is respectful and follows our guidelines, anyone can use it.

What kind of help do you provide?

We help with grammar, spelling, clarity, structure, organization, academic tone, argument development, feedback on drafts, formatting guidance (MLA, APA, Chicago, IEEE), summaries, and paraphrasing for clarity.

What don't you do?

To support academic integrity, we do not write full papers from scratch for submission, complete exams or graded assignments, or provide services intended to misrepresent authorship. We focus on helping users improve and learn from their own work.

Will my work be plagiarized or reused?

No. Your content is used only to provide feedback and writing support. We do not publish, sell, or reuse submitted material.

Do you store my files or personal information?

We only collect information necessary to respond to your request, such as your message, optional email, and uploaded files. We aim to keep data collection minimal and do not sell personal information. Please refer to the Privacy Policy for more details.

Can you help with citations and references?

Yes. We can help fix citation formatting, identify missing elements, improve reference list consistency, and guide you on proper citation styles. Users should always verify sources independently.

How fast do you respond?

Response times depend on demand. If you have a deadline, please mention it in your request so we can prioritize helpful feedback.

What should I include in my request to get the best help?

Include your topic, academic level, assignment instructions if available, your draft or outline, required citation style, and word count. Drafts help us give more accurate feedback.

Is this service affiliated with a university?

No. This is an independent writing support service. Users are responsible for following their institution's academic policies.

Do you check for AI writing?

We help improve clarity, tone, and natural writing style. We do not offer services intended to bypass AI detection systems.

How do I get started?

Visit the Essay Form page, fill in the required details, and submit your request. Uploading a draft is recommended for better feedback.
